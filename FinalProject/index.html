About Colby e-NABLE

Colby e-NABLE seeks to join the global e-NABLE community in providing free, customizable 3D printed upper limb assistive devices to those in need. Our team focuses on designing innovative adaptive devices and engaging with the Colby community.

Executive Summary

This club is structured in need-based way. Our Social Media Manager will set up a Facebook page, as is required to become an official e-NABLE chapter, and will respond to those who reach out to us in need. From there, we work with this individual to customize and build their prosthetic limb to their exact specifications, before finally giving them their new prosthetic. Our core objective is to provide a cheaper alternative to the very expensive prosthetics available today, and help those who may not be able to afford those steep prices still improve their quality of life.
